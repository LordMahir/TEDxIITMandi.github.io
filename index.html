EDUCATING THE EDUCATED

"EDUCATING THE EDUCATED" serves as the theme for this year's TEDx event. It focuses on the idea that education is a dynamic enlightening process and that the human mind is capable of challenging any norm and raising qualms about any practice it deems necessary. Eccentricity is a catalyst for innovation and development. It is a journey from "being to becoming," and goes beyond the mere idea of accumulating knowledge and abilities. There's always room to continue learning and growing, and it is indeed essential for individuals to continue seeking out newer information and perspectives rather than becoming complacent in their understanding. In terms of personal growth and development, this not only benefits the individual but also strengthens the society as a whole. In order to stay relevant and contribute significantly to their field or community, the said educated individuals must constantly adapt and expand their knowledge and skill sets. Furthermore, the theme also entails encouraging lifelong learning, self-awareness, and self-development in order to not only boost one's intellectual capacity, but also emotional, social, and spiritual development. It will be a guide to the act of thriving in the face of adversity and adapting to the change as the situation demands. We hope to unravel in this edition, how gradual transformation can help to uncover previously unexplored facets of life to reach our full potential.
